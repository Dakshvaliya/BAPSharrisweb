History

The BAPS Temple in Harris Park, NSW, is a landmark of spirituality and culture. Since its establishment, it has brought together the local community for worship, cultural events, and humanitarian efforts. The temple is part of the global BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha, which promotes values of peace, service, and harmony.
